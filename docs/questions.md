## Question 6 — Personal Limit

**What would make you step back permanently:**

A. *Becoming complicit in an injustice.*
B. *Acting without certainty about the consequences.*
C. *Losing emotional control over yourself.*
D. *Sacrificing your own balance for something beyond you.*

For each answer:
A → Action +1, Empathy +1
B → Action +1, Empathy −1

D → Action −1, Empathy −1
